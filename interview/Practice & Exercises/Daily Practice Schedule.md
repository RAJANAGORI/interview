## 🎯 Practice Tips

1. **Consistency:** Practice daily, even if just 15 minutes
2. **Out Loud:** Always practice explaining out loud
3. **Time Yourself:** Practice answering in 2-3 minutes
4. **Record:** Record yourself and review
5. **Feedback:** Get feedback from others when possible
6. **Refine:** Continuously improve your answers
7. **Confidence:** Build confidence through practice
