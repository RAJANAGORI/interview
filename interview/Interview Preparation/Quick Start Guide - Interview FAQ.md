# Quick Start Guide — Interview FAQ

> **How to use this module:** Short answers if an interviewer asks **how you study** or **how you stay current**. Pair with `Quick Start Guide.md`.

---

### Q1: How do you organize security interview preparation?

**Answer:** I use a **structured syllabus**: fundamentals first (identity, web platform), then **vulnerabilities**, then **design and cloud**. I mix **reading** with **spoken practice** and **spaced repetition** using quick references—not passive highlighting.

---

### Q2: How do you avoid memorizing lists without understanding?

**Answer:** I explain each topic **as if teaching someone else**, tie it to **past incidents or PR reviews**, and ask **“what breaks if this control fails?”**—that surfaces real understanding fast.

---

### Q3: What do you do when you don’t know an answer in an interview?

**Answer:** I **clarify scope**, state **assumptions**, outline a **reasonable investigation** (docs, tests, threat model), and connect to something **adjacent** I do know—without bluffing.
