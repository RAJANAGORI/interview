> **How to use this module:** Meta questions about **discipline**, **time management**, and **prioritization**. Pair with `Study Plan.md`.

---

### Q1: How do you prioritize what to study with limited time?

**Answer:** I align to the **role** (e.g. product security vs pure AppSec), map **gaps** from the job description, and schedule **high-leverage** topics first—usually **identity**, **TLS/HTTP**, **authZ**, **threat modeling**, and **one cloud** surface deeply rather than ten shallowly.

---

### Q2: What does an effective weekly study rhythm look like?

**Answer:** **Mix** deep reading with **active recall** and **mock questions**. I reserve one block for **review-only** (quick references) so earlier weeks don’t decay.

---

### Q3: How do you measure readiness?

**Answer:** Can I **explain** a topic in two minutes? Can I **map** it to a **system I built**? Can I handle a **follow-up** without freezing? If yes for most core topics, I shift to **mock interviews** and **stories**.
