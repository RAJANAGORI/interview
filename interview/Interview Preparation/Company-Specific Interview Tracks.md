# Company-Specific Interview Tracks

Use this as a role-loop adapter on top of the core topic library.

## Amazon (AppSec/Product Security)

- Emphasis: leadership principles, risk tradeoffs, ownership under ambiguity.
- Practice focus: concise writing, prioritization rationale, incident narratives.
- Pair modules: `Risk Prioritization and Security Metrics`, `Secure CI CD Pipeline Security`, `Business Logic Abuse and Fraud Threats`.

## Google (Security Engineer / Product Security)

- Emphasis: systems thinking, design depth, correctness, scalable controls.
- Practice focus: system-design-for-security prompts + threat modeling rigor.
- Pair modules: `System Design for Security`, `Threat Modeling`, `IAM and Least Privilege at Scale`.

## Meta (Product Security)

- Emphasis: fast iteration with measurable risk reduction.
- Practice focus: abuse scenarios, cross-functional influence, practical mitigations.
- Pair modules: `Web Cache Poisoning and Deception`, `Authorization and Authentication`, `Security-development-collaboration`.

## Security vendors (CrowdStrike / Palo Alto / etc.)

- Emphasis: detection efficacy, ATT&CK fluency, endpoint/cloud telemetry.
- Practice focus: adversary behavior mapping and incident-response judgment.
- Pair modules: `MITRE ATTACK Interview Fluency`, `EDR Evasion Awareness and Defense`, `Security Observability and Detection Engineering`.

## Fintech / banking security loops

- Emphasis: resilience, fraud abuse resistance, compliance-aware architecture.
- Practice focus: authentication hardening, PKI, incident drills.
- Pair modules: `PKI Program Design`, `Business Logic Abuse and Fraud Threats`, `Vulnerability Management Lifecycle`.

## Startup product security loops

- Emphasis: pragmatic controls with limited resources.
- Practice focus: high-leverage guardrails and roadmap sequencing.
- Pair modules: `Secure CI CD Pipeline Security`, `Software Supply Chain Security`, `System Design for Security`.
