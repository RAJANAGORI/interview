# Critical Clarification — Security Metrics and OKRs

## Misconception 1: “More findings means better security”

**Truth:** High **signal** findings can rise when detection improves—it’s not automatically bad. Judge **exploitability**, **exposure**, and **trend** of **material** risk—not raw counts.

---

## Misconception 2: “OKRs should be 100% secure”

**Truth:** OKRs should be **measurable** and **achievable** with trade-offs acknowledged—**residual risk** is normal; document it.

---

## Misconception 3: “MTTR alone measures response quality”

**Truth:** **MTTR** without **severity** and **customer impact** context can hide sloppy triage—or punish teams for transparent reporting.
