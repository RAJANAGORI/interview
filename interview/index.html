Interview Preparation

Hello! This is one of my projects that I keep updating with the latest information to help people prepare for interviews.

I cover all major topics in this open-source work, with few sub-sections for each topic:

  1. Content for preparation
  1. Scenario-based questions to best of my knowledge.

I hope it will make a positive impact on your interview and help you land into your dream job. I wish you all the best.

A few pieces of advice, always remember:

  • If you get rejected from any interview, don't worry. There are endless jobs and endless companies who need employees to run their business, so keep your confidence up.
  • If an interviewer disrespects you or makes fun of your answer, stay calm and don't react. The world is small and one day you might be interviewing that person, or they might appreciate your work online.
  • If you don't know the answer, let it go. You don't need to know everything. We're humans, not machines with databases.
  • Don't expect to receive a call or email after you apply for a job. Just apply and forget about it. If it's meant for you, it will come to you anyway.
