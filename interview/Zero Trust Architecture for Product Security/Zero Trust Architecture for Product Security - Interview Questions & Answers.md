# Zero Trust Architecture for Product Security - Interview Questions & Answers

## 1) How do you start zero-trust in an existing product?
Start with identity hardening, service-to-service auth, and least-privilege policy on critical APIs before full segmentation.

## 2) What failures happen most?
Policy sprawl, unclear ownership, and production outages due to untested deny rules.

## 3) How do you prove progress?
Track reduction in broad network trust paths, policy coverage for critical services, and unauthorized access blocks.
