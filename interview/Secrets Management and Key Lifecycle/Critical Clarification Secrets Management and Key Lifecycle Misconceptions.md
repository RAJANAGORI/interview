# Critical Clarification Secrets Management and Key Lifecycle Misconceptions

- "Vault/KMS adoption means problem solved."  
  Without rotation, monitoring, and IAM hygiene, secret risk remains high.

- "Long-lived API keys are okay for internal systems."  
  Internal compromise paths are common; short-lived credentials are safer.

- "Rotation once a year is enough."  
  Rotation frequency should match blast radius and threat model.
