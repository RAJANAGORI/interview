# Software Supply Chain Security - Interview Questions & Answers

## 1) How do you prioritize dependency vulnerabilities?
Prioritize by runtime reachability, exploit maturity, internet exposure, and business criticality, not just CVSS.

## 2) Where do SBOMs fail in practice?
Teams generate them but do not enforce deploy-time policy checks or ownership for remediation.

## 3) What is a strong staff-level goal?
100% production artifacts signed, provenance-verified, and traceable to reviewed source commits.
